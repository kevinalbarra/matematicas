VI International Conference / Days of Applied Mathematics

Nov 4 - 6, 2020

Conference Information

The VII International Conference / Days of Applied Mathematics, has as its main purpose to share concrete experiences of mathematical knowledge in the resolution of situations, seen from different contexts. In this sense, we invite the academic community (educators and students) to present their own work in oral or poster mode.
